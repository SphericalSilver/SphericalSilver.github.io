Here, I've compiled a list of useful Dataset sources you can browse through when trying to select a topic for your next project.

Datasets

  • Awesome Public Datasets. This behemoth of a compilation contains an amazing variety of Datasets covering a broad range of topics. If you can't think of the topic you want your next project to be based on, just give the categories here a look!
  • Datasets for Statistics. This is a Blog Post that links several useful Datasets you can use. While some of the links provided in this site don't work anymore, most of them still do!
  • data.gov.sg. Publicly available Singapore-oriented Datasets. If you're from a country other than Singapore, check if your own country has a data.gov equivalent.
  • r/datasets. The Datasets sub-reddit. Look up a dataset here, or ask someone to point you in the right direction!
  • journeyofanalytics Compilation. Compilation of Datasets put together on https://blog.journeyofanalytics.com!
  • Dataquest Blog. This blogpost on dataquest links several sources you can browse to look for a dataset of your choice.
  • Kaggle Datasets. Keep in mind that most Kaggle Datasets tend to be oriented around Machine Learning, and they often have most of the pre-processing already done! If you're looking to show off Data cleaning and processing skills starting from a raw dataset, you might want to look elsewhere!
