R Projects

International Champions Cup 2019 Twitter Analysis

Tweets containing the hashtags #ICC2019 and #ManUTD were mined using the Twitter API and the twitteR package in R. Fan sentiments around the ICC matches taking place in Singapore were analyzed! The results of the sentiment analysis were also used to train a classifier using the Naive Bayes algorithm that predicted, with good accuracy, whether a tweet was generally positive, or negative.

Analysis of the Link between the Perception of a School, and Academic Success of its Students.

Publicly available survey data was thoroughly cleaned, condensed, and then visualized to investigate as to whether the perceptions of schools by its students, teachers, and parents appeared correlated to the academic success of students from that school, using SAT scores as a measure for academic success. It was also investigated how the 3 groups of surveyed people differed in their perceptions.

Data Analysis of Forest Fire Occurrences in Portugal

Data on Forest Fires was explored and visualized to understand what variables contributed to the likelihood of a Forest Fire, to better be able to anticipate the possible occurrence of a Forest Fire. Variables that increased the severity of a Forest Fire, measured in terms of how many hectares of land were burned, were also explored extensively using boxplots, and the dataset was modified as needed to produce appropriate visualizations.
