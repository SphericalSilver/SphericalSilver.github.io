Useful Blogs and Articles to get you started

When starting out in a new field (such as I'm doing), the advice of people more experienced than yourself - who have been through the same journey - can be a colossally invaluable asset.

Whether it's to have a person you can confer with for sound advice, someone you can turn to for a nudge in the right direction, or even just a model of excellence for you to strive towards, the importance of the right kind of guiding hand can't be overstated!

I've put together a list here of some incredibly useful blogs and articles that do an outstanding job filling the role of a guiding mentor!

  • Dataquest Career guide. This is a multi-part blog series authored by the brilliant content creators at Dataquest. These blog posts go into great depth in explaining things like how to craft a resume, how to share a portfolio, preparing for an interview, negotiating job and salary offers, etc!
  • Journey of Analytics. This website hosted by experienced Data Scientist and Hiring Manager Anupama provides several useful learning resources, including links to various Datasets, sample project ideas, and an e-book on building a career in Data Science!
  • Guide on Data Visualization. This entry by JƒÅnis Gulbis goes into great length about what makes a visualization better! A great blog post that explains when you would want to use a particular type of graph!
  • Catalogue of Machine Learning Techniques. A curated list of applied machine learning and data science notebooks and libraries across different industries. This catalogue is a community effort by GitHub users, and as of this moment, is breathtakingly thorough!
